    Publications
    Analyzing and Mitigating Repetitions in Trip Recommendation[Link]
    Wenzheng Shu, Kangqi Xu, Wenxin Tai, Ting Zhong, Yong Wang, Fan Zhou
    SIGIR, 2024, CCF-A.

    Area: Trip Recommendation and Anti-Looping Theory

    We present AR-Trip, a cycle-aware framework designed for Point of Interest (POI) recommendation in Location-Based Social Networks (LBSNs). This framework incorporates three mechanisms: guiding, drifting, and adapting during both the training and decoding phases.
    Faithful Trip Recommender Using Diffusion Guidance (Student Abstract)[Link]
    Wenzheng Shu, Yanlong Huang, Wenxin Tai, Zhangtao Cheng, Bei Hui, Goce Trajcevski
    AAAI, 2024, CCF-A.

    Area: Faithful Trip Recommendation and Diffusion Models.

    Based on the Transformer architecture, we employ a conditional injection mechanism to progressively incorporate endpoint information during the inference process of the diffusion model, thereby ensuring the reliability of trajectory generation.
    Counterfactual Graph Learning for Anomaly Detection with Feature Disentanglement and Generation (Student Abstract)[Link]
    Yutao Wei, Wenzheng Shu, Zhangtao Cheng, Wenxin Tai, Chunjing Xiao, Ting Zhong
    AAAI, 2024, CCF-A.

    Area: Anomaly Detection, Counterfactual Sample Generation, and Graph Neural Network.

    A gradient-based separator is designed to decompose node features into class-specific features and environmental features. Subsequently, a variable-weight diffusion model is presented to combine class-specific features and environmental features of different nodes to generate counterfactual samples.
